Our Story

Strength Built
From the Ground Up

Founded over 30 years ago, Siripathi Metal Crushers has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted aggregate suppliers — serving thousands of construction projects with consistency, precision, and care.

We control every stage of production in-house: raw rock extraction, precision crushing, grading, and direct fleet delivery. No middlemen. No compromises.

The Siripathi Difference

Direct from Quarry

No middlemen. We quarry, crush, and deliver directly — ensuring quality control, price efficiency, and batch consistency every time.

Reliable Timelines

Our own transport fleet means we control delivery schedules. Your project timeline is our responsibility — we never miss a commitment.

Precision Grading

Our crushing process is calibrated to exact specifications. Every grade meets rigorous consistency standards — no surprises on site.

Expert Guidance

Unsure which grade you need? Our team advises on the right product and quantity for your specific project — from home builds to major infrastructure.

30 Years of Trust

Fair, Competitive Rates

Direct-from-quarry supply eliminates unnecessary markups. You get the best possible price without compromising on material quality.
